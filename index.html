NuChair Generation 4 Prototype Office Trial

You've been doing it wrong...

We've all been doing it wrong, sitting that is. But that's not our fault. We've only had one kind of chair - horizontal seat, back support, guaranteed to make you strain your lumbar in order to sit. And if it has a lumbar support, you need to lay back to get that to work. Even the most expensive chairs fit this formula. The result? Sitting is the new smoking. People are sitting more than ever now, and the resulting back pain is epidemic in proportion. In high-income countries, low back pain is the most frequent occupational health problem. It is also the most frequent activity-limiting complaint in the young and middle aged population and the second leading cause of sick leave.

But now there's NuChair!

NuChair is a revolutionary new chair that relieves back pain by eliminating the cause of the pain. NuChair eliminates the strain on your back that conventional chairs cause by maintaining your standing posture while you sit. Comfortably. Effortlessly. Automatically.

And the front support? Whoever made the first chair got it backwards! Everything you do while sitting is in front of you. Whether you're reaching for something on your desk, using your mouse and keyboard all day, or you're a Dentist or Optometrist (etc.) leaning forward toward a patient, you're putting strain on your back. NuChair's front support absorbs that strain and stress on your back, while still engaging the postural muscles that are dormant and atrophying in a conventional chair. NuChair also provides many other healthier advantages over conventional chairs, we'll explain those a bit later.

Conventional chair vs NuChair - Lumbar spine alignment

And there's much more..

NuChair eliminates the compression of the lungs and rib cage caused when sitting in conventional chair and expands the chest and lungs, resulting in deep, diaphragmatic breathing. This deeper breathing brings more oxygen to the body, making you more alert and energetic, you have more focus and energy, and improved immunity. You feel more peaceful and relaxed. More oxygen and no pain, you finish the day feeling refreshed. See what Posture Expert Dr. Steven Weiniger has to say about NuChair in the video above.

We've spent our budget on creating a well designed, high quality new product, made with materials designed to last a lifetime. A product that does what we promise it will do. Our mission is about the value that NuChair will bring to the millions of people we're going to help, whose lives we're going to change. Help us with that.

Something New - Tai Chi and NuChair

Qi Gong Stool

My sister introduced me to a college friend of hers, Jay, who now lives in Hong Kong. He has been involved with startups in the past, and has been giving me some advice in recent months on our NuChair startup. When I introduced Jay to NuChair, one of the first things he noticed was the angle of the seat. As a student of Tai Chi, he said he had seen this angle before. Jay just sent me these photos he took of his Tai Chi Master's Qi Gong stool. He explained, "FYI, the Qi Gong stool I told you about per ancient Chinese posture principle to allow the Qi (ether / air) go up and down the body straight through. One sits on it lotus style." This is the same posture and breathing one experiences in NuChair.

The connection between Tai Chi and NuChair is something that came up before when I met Sifu John, a Tai Chi Master in South Florida. He explained that one of the basic principals of Tai Chi regarding balance and breathing is how the posture and alignment centers over the hips. Sifu John told me that NuChair maintains that same posture when sitting, as he showed in the photo below.

Sifu John showing proper posture and alignment

Why does my back hurt when I sit?

When you sit in a conventional chair, your pelvis tucks under, causing your lumbar spine to bend forward. This compresses the front of the lumbar vertebrae, squeezing the discs between (see picture below). The disks become irritated, inflamed, and even damaged. All conventional chairs do this. NuChair eliminates this cause of lower back pain by maintaining the rearward curve of the lumbar vertebrae while you sit. Comfortably. Effortlessly. Automatically.
